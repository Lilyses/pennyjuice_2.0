Our Origin

Penny Juice is a family-owned juice concentrate brand founded in Iowa in 2001, offering easy-to-mix, colorful, and nutritious fruit juice blends specifically designed for childcare centers, preschools, and daycares across the U.S.

Being Our Best

Penny Juice stands out by offering affordable, easy-to-prepare juice concentrates with kid-friendly flavors, no added sugar, and options without artificial coloring specifically tailored to meet the nutritional needs and convenience demands of childcare centers, helping them comply with USDA guidelines while saving time and cost.
